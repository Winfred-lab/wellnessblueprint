No. This book is educational and explains the everyday systems behind fatigue, anxiety, sleep, gut health, burnout, and blood pressure. It isn't a substitute for professional diagnosis or care, and it says so clearly where it matters.

Do I need any special app to read this?

No. It's delivered as a PDF you can read on your phone, tablet, or computer — no app, no subscription, nothing extra to install.

How long will it take me to get through it?

Each chapter is a short, focused read (10–15 minutes) built around one question. Most readers finish the whole book in a few sittings, then use the 14-day plan alongside it.

Yes. The core explanations apply everywhere. A few sections, like food swaps and blood pressure context, include specific relevance for Nigerian and African readers, but the tools work regardless of where you live.

What if I don't finish the 14-day plan perfectly?

That's expected, and the book says so directly. Missing a day doesn't mean starting over — the plan is built to be picked back up, not followed perfectly.
